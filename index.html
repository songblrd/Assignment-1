Aristotle

Biography

Aristotle was an Ancient Greek philosopher. His writings covered a broad range of subjects, including the Arts, Philosophy, Math, Linguistics, Economics, Politics and Psychology. He has written many things for publication on these subjects, but only around a 3rd of these writings have survived. To this day his writings remain a subject of contemporary philosophical discussion around the world.
